Best NEET Coaching in Sikar (2026): What Our Listing Data Shows

Sikar's transformation into a national NEET hub stopped being a local story in 2024, when a student of the city's PCP (Prince Career Pioneer) scored a perfect 720/720 to take All India Rank 1. Families now weigh Sikar seriously against Kota — usually at meaningfully lower cost. Here is how the city's NEET institutes compare, using the ratings and facts on our own listing pages.

What the ratings say

On our platform, the most-reviewed NEET institute in Sikar is Navjeevan NEET Academy, a verified listing holding a 5.0 rating across 59 student reviews — the largest review base of any institute in the city. Gurukripa Career Institute, running since 2007, is also verified with a 5.0 from 23 reviews, and remains the city's most established full-scale player, with foundation courses, hostels and a scholarship test (GSAT) offering large fee waivers. Matrix rates 4.7 from 20 reviews, and the newer Path Career Institute — co-founded by faculty who feature in our podcast circuit's coverage of Sikar — holds a 5.0 from 19 early reviews.

The established names

PCP, the institute behind the 2024 AIR 1, has operated since 2009 and rates 4.2 from 10 reviews with us. CLC is the city's oldest, teaching since 1996 (4.5 from 11 reviews). National brands have arrived too: ALLEN and Sri Chaitanya both opened Sikar campuses in recent years, though their local review bases are still small.

How to choose between them

Ratings are a screen, not a verdict — especially where review counts are small. Shortlist two or three institutes, then visit each and ask to sit in the actual batch you would join. Ask who specifically will teach your batch, what the batch size is, and how doubt sessions work. Check the hostel situation at the same time: most institutes cluster around Piprali Road, and Sikar's hostels list room plans from roughly ₹8,000–15,000 a month. A two-day visit answers questions no website can.
