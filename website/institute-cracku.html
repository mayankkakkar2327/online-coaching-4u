About Cracku

Cracku is an online CAT prep platform founded in 2014 by IIT-IIM alumni and led by Maruti Konduri (IIT Bombay, IIM Ahmedabad) — a five-time CAT 100-percentiler (2019, 2021–2024). Its system is built around free Daily Targets: short VARC, DILR and Quant tests released every day with video solutions recorded by Konduri himself, with streak rewards for consistency. The paid CAT 2026 course adds 25,000+ practice questions arranged as adaptive topic tests that get harder as you answer correctly, live classes, and the DashCAT mock series — 20 national-level full mocks calibrated to real CAT difficulty, 5 gentler 'Headstart' mocks for early prep, and 45 sectional tests, with faculty attempting DashCATs live to demonstrate strategy. Cracku suits self-paced aspirants who want a daily plan and data-heavy practice over classroom timetables.
