About Navjeevan NEET Academy

Navjeevan NEET Academy is a NEET-focused coaching institute on Piprali Road, Sikar, launched by the Navjeevan Science Group — an education group that has run schools and JEE/defence coaching in Sikar for over 17 years, led by director Shankar Lal Bagaria. The academy operates from SB Tower on Piprali Road, in the heart of Sikar's coaching cluster. On our platform it holds a 5.0 rating from 59 student reviews — the largest review base of any coaching institute in Sikar — and its details are verified with the institute. Being NEET-only, the entire timetable, test series and faculty bench are built around the medical entrance rather than split across exams.
