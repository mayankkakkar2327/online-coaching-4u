About ALLEN Career Institute

ALLEN Career Institute's Sikar campus, near Piprali Circle on the Jhunjhunu Bypass, is the local outpost of India's best-known medical and engineering coaching brand, founded in Kota in 1988. The Sikar centre runs classroom programs for JEE and NEET on the same academic system as ALLEN's Kota headquarters — same study material, test series and batch structure. For Sikar students, it offers the ALLEN system without relocation to Kota. Its local review base on our platform is still small, so treat the national brand's reputation and a campus visit as your main evidence.
