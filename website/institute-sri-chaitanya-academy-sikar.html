About Sri Chaitanya Academy

Sri Chaitanya Academy's Sikar centre, opened in 2024 in Jat Colony off Piprali Road, brings one of India's largest education groups into the city's coaching market. The Sri Chaitanya group, which originated in Andhra Pradesh and runs one of the country's biggest school-plus-coaching networks, is nationally known for its structured JEE and NEET programs and integrated foundation model. The Sikar campus teaches JEE and NEET batches. As a recent entrant it has only a handful of local reviews so far, so aspirants should judge it on a campus visit and demo class rather than ratings alone.
