About JK Shah Online

JK Shah Online is the online arm of JK Shah Classes, a national CA coaching brand founded in 1983 and led by CA Vishal Shah. It offers CA Foundation, Intermediate and Final, CS courses and a B.Com-with-CA track, with recorded HD lectures, chapter-and-subject-wise access, structured study plans and doubt-solving support. For cities like Visakhapatnam with fewer dedicated local CA institutes, its established brand trust and fully online delivery make it a practical option, backed by faculty including Akshat Vakharia, CA Deep Jain, CA Mohit Jain and Chirag Thakkar on various subjects.
