About Kayam Girls Hostel

Kayam Girls Hostel is a student hostel in Piprali Road, Sikar, offering accommodation for girl students. Located at Piprali Rd, near Aksa Masjid, Sikar, Rajasthan, it is positioned close to the city's main coaching hubs, which keeps daily travel time short for students. Room plans and pricing are shared directly by the hostel on enquiry. Before booking, we recommend visiting in person to check the rooms, mess, security arrangements and house rules.
