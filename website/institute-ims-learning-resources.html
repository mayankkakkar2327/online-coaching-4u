About IMS Learning Resources

IMS Learning Resources, founded in Mumbai in 1977, is among India's oldest test-prep institutes and runs roughly 90+ centres across 50+ cities alongside full online CAT programs. Its SimCAT mock series is one of India's most widely taken CAT test series — which matters because percentile benchmarks are only as good as the test-taker pool behind them. The SimCAT 2026 packages go up to 40 full-length mocks with detailed video solutions, 30 sectional tests, and AI-powered performance insights that benchmark you against a national percentile and generate personalised improvement suggestions. Beyond CAT, IMS covers MH-CET, GMAT, GRE, CLAT and IPMAT, and pairs test prep with B-school selection mentoring — a legacy strength from five decades in admissions counselling.

Highlights

  • Founded 1977 — among India's first CAT test-prep institutes
  • SimCAT 2026: up to 40 full mocks with AI insights and national percentile benchmarking
  • One of India's largest mock test-taker pools, making percentiles meaningful
  • 90+ centres in 50+ cities plus complete online programs
