About EG Classes for IAS

EG Classes for IAS is a coaching institute in Mukherjee Nagar, Delhi, preparing students for IAS / PCS, RAS, BPSC, UPPSC, HPPSC, HPSC, UKPSC and MPPSC. It was established in 2017.
