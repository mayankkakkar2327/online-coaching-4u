Coaching Institutes in Sikar

15 listed · sorted by student rating by default. All information shown is baked into this page — nothing hidden behind loading spinners.

Sikar, in Rajasthan's Shekhawati region, has grown into one of India's fastest-rising coaching destinations — a serious alternative to Kota for NEET and JEE preparation at meaningfully lower fees and living costs. The city's institutes cluster along Piprali Road and Nawalgarh Road, surrounded by hostels, libraries and mess facilities built for its large outstation student population. Sikar's breakout moment came in 2024, when a student of the city's PCP institute scored a perfect 720/720 in NEET UG to take All India Rank 1. Beyond JEE and NEET, the city also hosts specialist academies for SSC, defence (NDA/CDS) and civil-services preparation. Frequently asked questions

Which is the best NEET coaching in Sikar?

By student ratings on our platform, Navjeevan NEET Academy (5.0 from 59 reviews) and Gurukripa Career Institute (5.0 from 23 reviews, running since 2007) lead, with Path Career Institute (5.0 from 19 reviews, founded 2025 by senior ex-Gurukripa faculty) the strongest new entrant. PCP, whose student scored NEET 2024's perfect 720/720 AIR 1, is the city's most results-proven name. Visit your top two before deciding — ratings are a screen, not a verdict.

Which is the best JEE coaching in Sikar?

Matrix Academy (founded 2014 by IIT Kharagpur alumni, 4.7 rating) and Gurukripa (since 2007) have the city's strongest JEE track records; Kautilya IIT-JEE Academy is the notable JEE-specialist. CLC, teaching since 1996, and national brands ALLEN and PW Vidyapeeth also run JEE batches in Sikar.

Is Sikar better than Kota for NEET preparation?

It depends on the student. Kota offers unmatched scale and competition density; Sikar offers lower costs, smaller batches and closer faculty access, with results that now compete at the top (NEET 2024's AIR 1 came from Sikar). Self-driven students often prefer Kota's ecosystem; students who benefit from supervision and a calmer environment often do better in Sikar.
