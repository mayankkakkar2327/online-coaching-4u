About Unacademy CAT

Unacademy CAT is the MBA entrance category on Unacademy, one of India's largest online learning platforms. Rather than one faculty team, it aggregates many independent top educators — including CAT 100-percentilers — teaching live classes daily across Quant, VARC and LRDI, with recorded courses, structured CAT 2026 batches (including Early Start batches for long-runway aspirants), and notes, slides and handouts bundled into the subscription. Access works on two tiers: the Plus subscription covers all live and recorded content, while Iconic adds a personal coach and one-on-one doubt-solving. A meaningful amount of content is free to watch before subscribing. Unacademy's real strength is choice — aspirants can sample several teaching styles and follow the educators that click, all inside one subscription.

Highlights

  • Aggregates many independent top educators, including CAT 100-percentilers
  • Plus and Iconic tiers — Iconic adds a personal coach and 1:1 doubt-solving
  • CAT 2026 Early Start batches for aspirants beginning preparation early
  • Switch freely between educators inside one subscription
