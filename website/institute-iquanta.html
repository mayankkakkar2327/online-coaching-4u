About iQuanta

iQuanta is an online CAT coaching platform founded in 2019 by Indrajeet Singh — the 'Wizard of Quant', who built his reputation solving aspirants' doubts in minutes inside India's largest CAT Facebook community, now over 4 lakh members strong. That community remains its signature: doubt resolution runs 24/7 alongside live concept and application classes with daily 15–30 minute practice quizzes. The CAT 2026 program includes 750+ tests with detailed solutions and a mock series of 20 proctored plus 15 unproctored full-length CATs calibrated to the real exam's difficulty and topic mix. After each mock, an AI analytics engine breaks down accuracy and time per question, estimates your percentile, and even suggests which B-schools your current level is likely to convert. iQuanta has also expanded into IPMAT and CLAT preparation.

Highlights

  • 24/7 doubt resolution backed by a 4-lakh+ member community
  • 35 full-length CAT mocks (20 proctored + 15 unproctored) with AI analytics
