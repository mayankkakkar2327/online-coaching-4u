South India's Chartered Accountancy coaching market spans everything from decades-old Chennai institutes with three generations of students to Kerala-wide commerce chains with 80-plus branches and national online-first brands serving cities with fewer local options. Below are ten CA coaching institutes drawn from Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Visakhapatnam, Kochi, Thiruvananthapuram, Coimbatore and Mysuru, profiled one by one — faculty, when they were established, mode of teaching, availability, official website, what the institute is about, its highlights and what students say. These profiles are compiled from the institutes' own pages and public information; always confirm current fees, batch timings and faculty directly with the institute before enrolling.

1. iProledge Academy

Faculty: CA Prudhvi Raj, CA Veeraj, CA Varshith Gowda, CA Tejas Sudhakar, CA Sharanya and CA Theja G Shastri.

Establised in which year: 2010–2011.

Mode of teaching: Online, offline and hybrid.

Availability: Basavanagudi, Bengaluru.

Official website link: iproledge.com

About the institute: A 15-plus-year Bengaluru commerce coaching institute covering CA Foundation, Intermediate and Final along with CS, CMA India, CMA USA and ACCA, built around concept-based teaching and mentoring.

HIghlights: Long operating history in Bengaluru, multi-exam faculty bench, and regular mock tests paired with structured mentoring.

Student reviews: Approximately 4.9/5 on Google, with reviews praising faculty, mentoring and mock-test quality.

2. SuccessEdge Academy

Faculty: Expert faculty team; the institute positions itself around a mentoring model with regular mock tests.

Establised in which year: 2012.

Mode of teaching: Online and offline.

Availability: Three Bengaluru centres — Vijayanagar, Basavanagudi and Malleshwaram.

Official website link: successedge.co

About the institute: Runs CA Foundation, Intermediate and Final coaching alongside CS, CMA and ACCA programs across three Bengaluru centres, built around a mentoring model with expert faculty and regular mock tests, in both online and offline formats.

HIghlights: Three Bengaluru centres under one brand, broad course spread beyond CA alone, and a mentoring-led teaching model.

Student reviews: Testimonials highlight faculty who simplify difficult concepts and mock-test practice; also profiled on UrbanPro as a professional-course coaching provider in the city.

3. KS Academy

Faculty: Founded and led by CA K. Saravanan, with a faculty team the institute describes as having a high level of concern for students, across its Chennai, Coimbatore and Hyderabad centres.

Establised in which year: 2010.

Mode of teaching: Online, offline and "KS Virtuals" live-online classes.

Availability: Chennai (Tambaram), Coimbatore, Hyderabad (Ameerpet) and Bengaluru, with a combined CA + B.Com route alongside ACCA and allied programs through group entities.

Official website link: ksacademy.co.in

About the institute: One of South India's best-known CA coaching brands, started in Chennai and expanded into a multi-city network covering CA Foundation, Intermediate and Final.

HIghlights: Strong brand recall across five South Indian cities, combined CA + B.Com track, and a consistent faculty structure repeated across all its branches.

Student reviews: Social and student feedback is generally positive, with the official site and social pages emphasising close faculty attention; branch-specific review depth varies.

4. CMS for CA

Faculty: Experienced CA/CMA faculty team; the institute claims decades of combined teaching experience.

Establised in which year: Not clearly published by the institute.

Mode of teaching: Offline, integrated with a college-style model, alongside likely blended online support.

Availability: Sanjeeva Reddy Nagar and other campuses in Hyderabad.

Official website link: cmsforca.in

About the institute: A large Hyderabad commerce college and professional academy claiming 12,000-plus qualified CA/CMA professionals and 500-plus all-India ranks, offering CA, CMA, CS and integrated MEC/CEC, CLAT, IPMAT and CUET coaching.

HIghlights: Large scale and claimed track record, broad program spread beyond CA alone, and Shiksha-rated faculty.

Student reviews: Shiksha reviews rate faculty around 4.1/5, with students praising study material and weekly exams alongside some mixed feedback typical of a large multi-program institute.

5. JK Shah Online

Faculty: Led by CA Vishal Shah, with subject faculty including Akshat Vakharia, CA Deep Jain, CA Mohit Jain and Chirag Thakkar.

Establised in which year: 1983.

Mode of teaching: Fully online, with recorded HD lectures and demo lectures available before enrolling.

Availability: Pan-India online access, including Visakhapatnam and other cities with fewer dedicated local CA institutes.

Official website link: online.jkshahclasses.com

About the institute: The online arm of JK Shah Classes, a national CA coaching brand offering CA Foundation, Intermediate and Final, CS courses and a B.Com-with-CA route, with chapter and subject-wise lecture access and structured study plans.

HIghlights: More than four decades of brand history, fully online delivery model, and recorded lectures that let students revisit difficult topics on their own schedule.

Student reviews: Positioned as an established, trusted brand; useful specifically for cities where dedicated local CA institutes are limited.

6. Logic School of Management

Faculty: Faculty team spanning CA, CS, CMA and ACCA across the institute's branch network.

Establised in which year: 2005 (institute cites 20–21 years of experience).

Mode of teaching: Blended offline and online.

Availability: Head office in Palarivattom, Kochi, with a claimed 82-plus branches across Kerala, including Thiruvananthapuram.

Official website link: logiceducation.org

About the institute: One of Kerala's largest commerce professional institutes, covering CA Foundation, Intermediate and Final alongside CS, CMA India, CMA USA, ACCA, CPA, EA and integrated B.Com/M.Com programs.

HIghlights: Very large branch network for a regional institute, broad course spread, and a high self-reported recommendation rate.

Student reviews: The institute's Facebook page shows a 94% recommendation rate; it is also listed as an established institute on UrbanPro, with generally positive public feedback.

7. Rank Centre for CA

Faculty: Experienced faculty team; the institute describes its faculty as rich in experience.

Establised in which year: Institute cites 20-plus years of experience.

Mode of teaching: Online and offline, in a Kerala centre-based model.

Availability: Kerala, with presence relevant to Thiruvananthapuram students.

Official website link: rankcentreforca.com

About the institute: A Kerala-focused CA-only coaching centre covering CA Foundation, Intermediate and Final, positioned around two decades of focused CA exam support.

HIghlights: CA-only focus rather than a multi-program commerce academy, and a long operating history within Kerala's CA coaching space.

Student reviews: Official positioning is strong; independent, publicly available review detail is more limited than for larger multi-city brands, so a direct enquiry is worth it before enrolling.

8. PR Academy

Faculty: Industry-veteran faculty, described by the institute as highly qualified instructors.

Establised in which year: Not clearly published by the institute.

Mode of teaching: Offline, with likely online support.

Availability: Ram Nagar, Coimbatore, with on-campus hostel facilities for outstation students.

Official website link: pracademy.in

About the institute: "Professionalism Redefined" — a Coimbatore commerce academy offering a structured, level-wise CA curriculum alongside CMA and ACCA, with regular mock tests and doubt-clearing sessions.

HIghlights: Structured CA-through-Final curriculum, on-site hostel facilities for outstation students, and a multi-course offering beyond CA alone.

Student reviews: Student testimonials on the institute's own site consistently mention faculty attention and a supportive, motivating environment; independent third-party review volume is limited.

9. ProTree Academy

Faculty: CS Aarti Panigrahi, CS Pranab Panigrahi, CA Abhishek Hinchegeri, CS Manish Jain, CS Anuj Mehta, CA Niraj Jain and CS Harsha Jain.

Establised in which year: Not clearly published; the institute cites six years of academic operation.

Mode of teaching: Offline, with likely online support.

Availability: Mysuru, Karnataka, with additional location pages across Karnataka including Bengaluru, Bellary, Hubli and others.

Official website link: protreeacademy.com

About the institute: A Mysuru-based CA and CS coaching academy emphasising personalised guidance and a structured curriculum, running CA Foundation, Intermediate and Final alongside CSEET, CS Executive and CS Professional.

HIghlights: Dual CA-and-CS focus, a faculty team with both corporate and academic experience, and an actively maintained blog with frequent 2026 updates.

Student reviews: The institute's site publishes detailed named testimonials from CS and CSEET students; independent third-party review data remains more limited.

10. RR Academy

Faculty: Experienced faculty team; the institute emphasises small batches and personal attention.

Establised in which year: 1993.

Mode of teaching: Offline, with online support.

Availability: Chennai, Tamil Nadu.

Official website link: rracademy.in

About the institute: An established Chennai professional-coaching academy with more than 30 years of CA, CS and CMA/ICWA/ACS coaching and all-India rank claims among past students.

HIghlights: Long operating history, a small-batch teaching model, and weekly test cycles.

Student reviews: The institute's website publishes detailed testimonials praising experienced faculty, weekly tests and conceptual clarity.

Choosing between these ten

Course fees, batch structure and current faculty lineups change over time and are often not published city-wise, so treat every detail above as a starting point for a direct conversation with the institute rather than a locked-in fact. Our general institute-selection checklist — sit in a real class, get a written refund policy, verify at least one named result — applies just as well across CA coaching as it does for any other exam.
