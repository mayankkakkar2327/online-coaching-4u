IAS Coaching in Delhi: Choosing Between the Big Names and the New Wave

Delhi remains the geographic centre of UPSC preparation, and two localities anchor it: Old Rajinder Nagar and Mukherjee Nagar. But the choice inside those localities has changed shape — legacy institutes now compete with newer, mentor-driven academies, and the trade-offs are worth understanding before you sign anything.

The legacy tier

The old guard is exemplified by Vajiram and Ravi, teaching since 1976 and still the institute against which others in Old Rajinder Nagar measure themselves (it rates 4.8 from early reviews on our platform). Legacy institutes offer deep faculty benches, time-tested study material and huge alumni networks. Their weakness is scale: classes can be very large, and personal mentorship is limited unless you seek it out.

The newer wave

Among newer academies on our Delhi listings, Tathastu ICS (founded 2023, near Rajendra Place) carries a 4.9 rating from 14 reviews — the highest-rated IAS listing we have in Delhi — while verified institutes like EG Classes in Mukherjee Nagar (4.6 from 20 reviews) and PATH IAS in Old Rajinder Nagar (4.8) build their pitch on smaller batches and closer mentorship. The newer institutes trade brand history for accessibility of faculty — a trade many aspirants, especially those on a second or third attempt, consider worth making.

Locality matters more than you think

Old Rajinder Nagar and Mukherjee Nagar are ecosystems, not just addresses: libraries, test series centres, photocopy shops with every toppers' notes, and thousands of fellow aspirants. Living inside one compresses your logistics to a ten-minute walk. The cost is higher rent and a pressure-cooker atmosphere that suits some and corrodes others. Aspirants preparing alongside a job increasingly skip the localities entirely and go online — a route that has produced plenty of selections.

The one-week test

Most Delhi institutes will let you attend classes for a few days before committing, or at least sit one full session. Use it. Check the batch size claim against the room in front of you, ask current students how doubt sessions actually work, and read the refund terms before paying. A UPSC attempt costs a year of your life; spend a week choosing where it happens.
