About Vajiram and Ravi

Vajiram and Ravi is a coaching institute in Old Rajinder Nagar, Delhi, preparing students for IAS / PCS. Founded in 1976, it is one of the longest-running institutes in the city with more than 50 years of teaching experience. Students rate it 4.8/5 based on 9 reviews on our platform. The institute is located at 9-B, Bada Bazaar Marg, Old Rajinder Nagar, New Delhi 110060. We recommend visiting the campus and sitting in on a demo class before enrolling.
