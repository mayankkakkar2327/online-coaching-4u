Frequently asked questions

Which is the best online CAT coaching in India for 2026?

Rodha stands out as the only platform with its complete CAT syllabus (1,400+ videos) free on YouTube, so you can verify the teaching quality before paying anything; its paid batches and harder-than-CAT mock series stay cheaper than legacy institutes. Strong alternatives by need: Cracku for app-based daily-target prep by a five-time 100-percentiler, iQuanta for 24/7 doubt support, and IMS for India's most widely taken mock series (SimCAT).

Which online CAT coaching is free or cheapest?

Rodha offers the most complete free preparation — its entire video syllabus is on YouTube, plus free full-length and sectional mocks with video solutions. Cracku's Daily Targets are free every day with video solutions. Hitbullseye runs one of the largest free practice portals. Among paid full courses, MBA Wallah (Physics Wallah) and Rodha are typically the most affordable.

Which CAT mock test series should I take for CAT 2026?

IMS SimCAT has India's largest test-taker pool, which makes its percentiles the most meaningful benchmark. Rodha's mocks are deliberately 10–15% harder than the real exam on a TCS iON-style interface — good for building endurance. Cracku's 20 DashCATs and iQuanta's 35 mocks with AI analytics are also credible. Serious aspirants often combine two series in the final months.

Is online coaching enough to crack CAT?

Yes — online-prepared students score 99+ percentiles every year, and CAT itself is a computer-based exam, so online mock practice transfers directly. The trade-off is accountability: classroom programs impose structure, while online prep demands self-discipline. Platforms compensate with daily targets (Cracku), 24/7 communities (iQuanta) and fixed live schedules (Rodha, MBA Wallah).
