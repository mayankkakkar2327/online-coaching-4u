About CATKing Educare

CATKing Educare is a Mumbai-based MBA coaching institute founded by Sumit Singh Gandhi — an SPJIMR Mumbai PGDM graduate with a B.Tech from NIT Karnataka and Harvard executive education — who still personally teaches the live VARC classes. CATKing runs no franchisees: everything is delivered by the core team, with faculty drawn from IIM, SP Jain, NMIMS and JBIMS alumni. Its CAT 2026 lineup spans Intensive and Turbo courses through to dedicated repeater programs pitched at specific percentile bands, with live lectures, updated e-books and full-length mocks, and course validity extending into 2027 so a two-attempt plan is covered by one purchase. Its strongest differentiator remains Maharashtra depth: MH-CET coverage and non-IIM B-school strategy (SPJIMR, NMIMS, JBIMS) that national platforms treat as an afterthought.
