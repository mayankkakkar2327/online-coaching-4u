About MBA Pathshala

MBA Pathshala is a compact online CAT coaching platform founded by Udit Saini, a Quantitative Aptitude and LRDI educator with over eight years of teaching experience, with co-founder Suman Shekhawat — a VARC mentor previously ranked among the top CAT educators on Unacademy. The platform runs live and recorded CAT 2026 batches with mock tests and strategy sessions, covering MH-CET and other MBA entrances alongside CAT. Its pitch is deliberate smallness: a two-person core faculty students deal with directly, rather than a rotating bench — combined with pricing positioned well below the legacy institutes. Best suited to aspirants who value direct mentor access and dislike the anonymity of large-platform batches.
