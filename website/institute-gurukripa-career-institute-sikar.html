About Gurukripa Career Institute (GCI)

Gurukripa Career Institute (GCI) is Sikar's most established large-scale coaching institute, teaching NEET, IIT-JEE and foundation courses (classes 6–10) since 2007. Its head office is at Jyoti Nagar on Piprali Road, with additional campuses in Jaipur and Alwar. Gurukripa runs its own scholarship test, GSAT, through which students can earn fee waivers of up to 100%, and provides hostel and mess support for the large outstation population it draws from across North India. The listing is verified with the institute and rates 5.0 from 23 student reviews on our platform. For many families comparing Sikar options, GCI is the benchmark the newer institutes measure themselves against.

Highlights

  • Multiple campuses across Sikar, Jaipur and Alwar
  • GSAT scholarship test with up to 100% fee waiver
