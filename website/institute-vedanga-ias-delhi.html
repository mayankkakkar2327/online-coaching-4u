About Vedanga IAS

Vedanga IAS is a coaching institute in Laxmi Nagar, Delhi, preparing students for IAS / PCS, BPSC, UPPSC, UKPSC, HPSC and SPSC. It has been operating since 2011 and has built a steady local reputation over 15 years. Early student feedback is positive, though the review count is still small. The institute is located at U-135, Baba Complex, Laxmi Nagar, Delhi 110092. We recommend visiting the campus and sitting in on a demo class before enrolling.
