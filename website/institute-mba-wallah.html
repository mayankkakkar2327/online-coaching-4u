About MBA Wallah (Physics Wallah)

MBA Wallah is the MBA entrance vertical of Physics Wallah (PW), the edtech company famous for aggressive affordability. Its CAT 2026 lineup is a ladder of batches — from a low-cost Starter Kit through Pioneer, Pro and Elite to the mentorship-heavy Power batch and the Core batch that adds foundation-building for students starting from scratch. Depending on batch, features include two-way interactive live classes, unlimited recorded playback, daily practice problems with video solutions recorded by the teaching faculty, 15,000+ practice questions, hard-copy books, and in the Power batch, 1:1 mentorship. Weekend-only options serve working professionals. MBA Wallah is the natural pick for budget-conscious aspirants who want a full live-class ecosystem at a fraction of legacy-institute pricing.

Highlights

  • MBA vertical of Physics Wallah — the low-fee, high-volume edtech model applied to CAT
  • Batch ladder from budget Starter Kit to 1:1-mentorship Power batch
