Delhi remains one of India's biggest CAT preparation markets, with everything from long-running classroom institutes to newer online-first platforms competing for aspirants' attention. Below are ten options Delhi CAT aspirants come across most often, profiled one by one — faculty, when they were established, how they teach, availability, official website, what the institute is about, its highlights, fees and batches, and what students say. These profiles are compiled from the institutes' own pages and public information; always confirm current fees, batch timings and faculty directly with the institute before enrolling.

1. Rodha

Faculty: Primarily led by Ravi Prakash Rodha and a team of experienced instructors for Quantitative Aptitude, LRDI and VARC, with a strong focus on concept clarity.

Establised in which year: Recent‑generation online-first CAT coaching brand (active for CAT 2024–2026 cycles; exact incorporation year is mentioned in their company records).

Mode of teaching: Online‑only coaching with live classes, recordings, test-series and digital/physical study material; no physical offline centres in Delhi.

Availability: PAN‑India through online batches; multiple "Hinglish" comprehensive batches (for example, R3, R7) with fixed start dates and validity till the target CAT/OMET season.

Official website link: rodha.co.in

About the institute: Rodha is a dedicated CAT and OMET (XAT, SNAP, NMAT etc.) online coaching platform focused on building strong fundamentals through phased learning, live classes, recorded lectures, worksheets, tests and structured guidance from basics to advanced level.

HIghlights: Strong emphasis on core conceptual understanding, extensive YouTube and app-based free content, full live plus recorded coverage of QA, LRDI and VARC, inclusion of OMET courses and mocks with CAT batches, and booklets plus topic-wise tests and sectionals.

Fees and Batches: Comprehensive CAT 2026 "all‑in‑one" batches (covering QA, LRDI, VARC plus OMETs) are typically priced in the mid-range segment (exact fee varies by batch and offer); example batch schedules include daily live sessions with Quants classes (around 5 pm–7 pm on multiple weekdays), LRDI classes in late evening/night slots (around 8 pm–10 pm or 10 pm–12 am) and VARC classes (around 5 pm–7 pm) with course completion by around September for CAT 2026.

Student reviews: Students frequently praise the high-quality QA and LRDI lectures, depth of concept coverage, quick availability of class recordings and rich test ecosystem. Some students have also talked about the special batches for working professional have really been a great relief and has helped alot. Users have also been praising their new initiatives Rodha buddy app and Rodha panchayat alot for there 1:1 support and quick doubt solving.

2. T.I.M.E. (Triumphant Institute of Management Education)

Faculty: Experienced CAT faculty across Delhi branches; senior mentors for VARC, DILR and Quant (faculty varies by centre).

Establised in which year: 1992.

Mode of teaching: Classroom (multiple Delhi centres) and online/live classes.

Availability: Multiple Delhi branches (such as Connaught Place, Hudson Lane, Pitampura, Dwarka, Satya Niketan) with weekday and weekend batches; contact specific centre for current seat availability.

Official website link: time4education.com

About the institute: Pan‑India test-prep brand for MBA entrance exams offering structured classroom programs, comprehensive study material, test series and GD/PI support with a strong offline presence in Delhi.

HIghlights: Long-standing brand, wide branch network in Delhi, established CAT test series, specialized modules for each section and comprehensive GD/PI support.

Fees and Batches: Full CAT classroom or online course is generally in the range of about ₹50,000–₹75,000 depending on duration, centre and inclusions; weekday and weekend batches are available.

Student reviews: Overall positive for classroom experience, faculty and test series; some students mention that experience can vary by branch and individual faculty.

3. Career Launcher (CL)

Faculty: Combination of in-house and visiting mentors for Quant, VARC and DILR; senior faculty profiles are listed centre-wise and some centres have star faculty for specific sections.

Establised in which year: Organization founded in the mid‑1990s (national test-prep brand since late 1990s); Delhi centres opened subsequently (each centre has its own start year).

Mode of teaching: Classroom programs across multiple Delhi centres plus extensive online/live and recorded courses.

Availability: Centres in areas such as Connaught Place, Preet Vihar, Saket, Kailash Colony and others; weekday, weekend and sometimes night batches are run.

Official website link: careerlauncher.com

About the institute: Large national MBA coaching brand offering CAT classroom courses, online programs, test series, mentoring and GD/PI prep with structured course plans and material.

HIghlights: Strong brand recall, comprehensive classroom and online offerings, detailed test series, dedicated GD/PI modules and mentoring-driven premium programs.

Fees and Batches: CAT classroom and online programs usually fall in a band of roughly ₹55,000–₹80,000 depending on package and duration; multiple batch options (weekday, weekend, some night batches) are offered.

Student reviews: Appreciated for quality test series, structured material and mentoring; some students find the premium programs relatively expensive but value them for focused guidance.

4. IMS Learning Centre

Faculty: Experienced test-prep faculty for Quant, VARC and DILR with some mentors having decades of teaching experience; faculty is centre-specific.

Establised in which year: IMS as a brand has been active since the 1970s–1980s and has expanded into multiple cities including Delhi over time.

Mode of teaching: Classroom coaching at Delhi centres such as Rajouri Garden, Karol Bagh, GTB Nagar, etc., along with online classes and test-series.

Availability: Multiple centres across Delhi offering weekday and weekend batches; online test-series and programs are accessible pan‑India.

Official website link: imsindia.com (Delhi centres section)

About the institute: One of the oldest exam-prep companies in India, providing structured CAT coaching programs, detailed mock test-series and mentoring support.

HIghlights: Long history in MBA entrance coaching, comprehensive test-series platform, multiple Delhi centres and a mix of classroom plus online options.

Fees and Batches: Typical CAT course fees are generally reported around ₹50,000–₹75,000 depending on package, city and duration; batches are offered in both weekday and weekend formats.

Student reviews: Students often praise the test-series quality, analytics and structured preparation plans; some mention crowding in popular centres during peak season.

5. Arun Sharma's Mindworkzz

Faculty: Led by Arun Sharma (well‑known CAT Quant author) with senior faculty for Quant, LRDI and VARC; some sessions are taken by Arun himself along with a core team.

Establised in which year: Built over Arun Sharma's long-standing teaching career; Delhi classroom operations have been active for several years.

Mode of teaching: Classroom coaching in Delhi (notably Connaught Place / GTB Nagar type locations) plus online/live courses.

Availability: Limited-seat classroom batches with weekday and weekend options, as well as online batches catering to students across India.

Official website link: mindworkzz.com (and related official pages)

About the institute: Niche CAT coaching brand centered around Arun Sharma's books and teaching style, with a strong focus on Quant and problem-solving rigor.

HIghlights: High focus on Quant, author-curated material, challenging problem sets and targeted coaching for high-percentile aspirants.

Fees and Batches: Course fees for complete CAT programs are commonly in the range of about ₹60,000–₹80,000 depending on the mode and inclusions; smaller specialized batches and doubt sessions are run.

Student reviews: Quant training and quality of questions receive strong appreciation; some students prefer to supplement verbal preparation with other sources or coaches.

6. TathaGat (TG)

Faculty: Founder-led team (for example, Rajat and core faculty) with experienced mentors for all CAT sections and a reputation for close interaction with students.

Establised in which year: 2007.

Mode of teaching: Classroom coaching from its Delhi centre (such as Barakhamba Road) along with online classes and test-series.

Availability: Single or limited Delhi centres with carefully sized batches; online offerings for students outside Delhi.

Official website link: tathagat.co.in

About the institute: Boutique CAT coaching institute with a student-first approach, known for rigorous concept-building, homework and frequent interaction with faculty.

HIghlights: Small batch sizes, personalised attention, rigorous question practice and a tightly monitored learning environment.

Fees and Batches: Course fees are usually in the range of around ₹55,000–₹75,000 depending on the specific program; batches are generally structured as weekday or weekend intensive programs.

Student reviews: Often praised for personal attention, doubt-solving and serious peer group; limited seats mean batches fill early but also keep class sizes controlled.

7. MBAGuru

Faculty: Claims of 99+ percentile mentors heading different sections with a team that focuses on adaptive learning and performance tracking.

Establised in which year: Delhi-based coaching brand active for many years; detailed foundation year is provided on their official channels.

Mode of teaching: Classroom programs in Delhi plus online adaptive courses and test-series.

Availability: Centres in areas such as Connaught Place, Kailash Colony, Rajouri Garden and more, along with multiple online batches.

Official website link: mbaguru.in

About the institute: Delhi-headquartered CAT coaching institute known for its "adaptive" preparation model that adjusts difficulty and focus based on student performance, backed by structured classroom teaching.

HIghlights: Adaptive prep methodology, relatively smaller batches, detailed performance analytics and regular feedback for students.

Fees and Batches: CAT programs generally fall in a range of about ₹60,000–₹75,000; classroom batches run in weekday and weekend modes, and separate online batches are available.

Student reviews: Students often highlight personalised attention and adaptive approach as positives; a few compare the fees with bigger national brands while appreciating the Delhi-centric focus.

8. Alchemist (Alchemist Learning)

Faculty: Senior in‑house faculty team with specialisation across Quant, LRDI and VARC; focus on mentoring and one‑to‑one interactions.

Establised in which year: Has been operational in Delhi for several years; each centre may have its own start year.

Mode of teaching: Classroom programs with some blended/online support.

Availability: Centres in localities such as Pitampura and Connaught Place, with limited-size batches running through the year.

Official website link: alchemistindia.com (and related official pages)

About the institute: Delhi-focused CAT coaching institute that positions itself on the promise of personalised coaching and attention through smaller, mentor-driven batches.

HIghlights: Small batch sizes, individual doubt-solving, mentor support and emphasis on conceptual clarity over rote practice.

Fees and Batches: Fee range is usually reported around ₹60,000–₹90,000 depending on the course depth and duration; different weekday and weekend batch options are offered.

Student reviews: Students generally appreciate the personalised atmosphere and faculty access; some consider it slightly premium-priced for its boutique positioning.

9. AnkGanit

Faculty: Math-focused faculty with strong Quant backgrounds, offering intensive Quant training plus support for other sections via structured material.

Establised in which year: Operating in Delhi for several years; exact year is given on their detailed profiles.

Mode of teaching: Classroom coaching from its Delhi centre (often associated with Connaught Place) along with online/live Quant-focused offerings.

Availability: Limited-size classroom batches and online live courses; batch starting dates are scheduled across the year.

Official website link: ankganit.in

About the institute: Boutique coaching with a clear emphasis on Quantitative Aptitude (the name itself signifies mathematics), known for focused practice and high-level problem-solving.

HIghlights: Strong Quant specialization, intensive problem practice, personalised doubt clearing and focus on building speed plus accuracy.

Fees and Batches: Reported fee for full CAT preparation is roughly around ₹40,000 (may vary with inclusions and time); batch details and schedules are shared directly by the centre.

Student reviews: Frequently appreciated by students for strengthening Quant; many aspirants combine it with separate verbal coaching to have a balanced CAT prep strategy.

10. Kitabee CAT Preparation

Faculty: Classroom faculty with experience in CAT coaching and section-wise mentors for Quant, LRDI and VARC.

Establised in which year: Has been active in Delhi for multiple years; exact establishment year is mentioned on detailed institute profiles.

Mode of teaching: Classroom coaching (notably from Jhandewalan Extension and nearby areas) with online support and test-series.

Availability: Delhi centres with weekday and weekend batches that are relatively compact in size.

Official website link: kitabee.in (and related official pages)

About the institute: Delhi-based CAT coaching centre offering full‑length classroom programs, test-series and doubt-solving sessions at a relatively accessible price point.

HIghlights: Focus on classroom interaction, approachable faculty and structured test practice aimed at working professionals and students alike.

Fees and Batches: Course fees are commonly reported around ₹55,000 for the full program (subject to change); batch options usually include both weekday and weekend slots.

Student reviews: Reviews are generally mixed to positive, with students liking the classroom environment and affordability compared to some larger brands.

Choosing between these ten

Fees, batch structure and faculty availability all change over time, so treat every figure above as a starting point for a conversation with the institute, not a locked-in number. Our general institute-selection checklist — sit in a real class, get a written refund policy, verify at least one named result — applies just as well when choosing among these ten as it does anywhere else.
