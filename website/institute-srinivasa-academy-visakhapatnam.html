About Srinivasa Academy

Srinivasa Academy, established in 2009 by Dr. Vidhya Srikanth and associated with JK Shah Classes, offers CA, CMA and CS coaching. While based in Chennai rather than Visakhapatnam itself, its offline-plus-hybrid delivery makes it a relevant option where dedicated local CA coaching is limited, such as in Vizag. Reviews note friendly faculty and practical teaching examples, though students should verify current batch access and delivery format for their city before enrolling.
